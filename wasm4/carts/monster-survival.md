# Monster Survival

Zombie survival! Kill all zombies and collect all keys to finish the game!
Controls:
    Up – Move up
    Down – Move down
    Left – Move left
    Right – Move right
    Z – Throw poison orb
    X – Throw stone
    Left-click – Pause the game
